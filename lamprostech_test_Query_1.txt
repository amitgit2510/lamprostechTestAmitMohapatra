QUERY CODE


SELECT
  "to" AS receiver,
  SUM(value) AS total_tokens_received
FROM erc20_optimism.evt_Transfer
WHERE
  "from" = 0x5B8F3fb479571Eca6A06240b21926Db586Cdf10f
  AND contract_address = 0x4200000000000000000000000000000000000042
GROUP BY
  1
ORDER BY
  total_tokens_received DESC
LIMIT 10



INSIGHTS

1)WE FOUND THE TOP TEN RECEIVER WHO RECEIVED THE HIGHEST TOKEN FROM THE GIVEN ADDRESS AND CONTRACT ADDRESS

2) WE CAN CHECK THE AMOUNT OF TOKEN RECEIVED BY OUR SELECTED(TOP 10) RECEIVER
